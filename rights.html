Key rights

Refugees and migrants deserve fundamental protection.

Protection from harm

People seeking safety must be shielded from violence, exploitation, and forced return to danger.

Access to essentials

Food, shelter, education, and basic healthcare are part of a humane response.

Freedom from discrimination

No person should be denied dignity because of nationality, ethnicity, or migration status.
