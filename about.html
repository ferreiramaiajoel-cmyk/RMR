Our mission

We exist to remind communities that refugee rights are human rights.

Refugee and Migrants Rights is created for native and natural citizens who want to learn, reflect, and act with compassion. The goal is to build awareness that people who move from difficult places deserve equal respect and protection.

What guides us

  • Dignity over fear
  • Justice over prejudice
  • Inclusion over exclusion
